Committed To Making Your Special occasion a memorable one



I can do Mehndi/Henna for just about any occasion. I Specialize in Bridal Mehndi, Sangeet Parties, Engagements, Baby Showers, Birthday Parties, Ladies Night outs, School Events, Corporate Events and Fundraisers. I love to extend this art form onto cakes and other mediums like wood, glass and candles.


Bridal Mehndi A whole range of bridal design options available, from simple to intricate with heavy details, from traditional Indian to Arabic. Packages depend on the style and length of the mehendi on arms and legs.


Sangeet Parties Whether it is mehndi or sangeet night, kick off the wedding festivities with mehndi fun. The pricing is based on an hourly rate and not on a per hand rate.


Engagements Adorn your beautiful hands for the first wedding festivity with some mehndi.


Baby/Bridal Showers Whether it’s belly mehndi or mehndi for your guests at your shower, it’s sure to be a hit.


Birthday Parties Who says mehndi is just for weddings, bring the joy of color to any event.


School/Corporate Events Mehndi is always a super hit at all school and corporate functions.
